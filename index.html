/ ABOUT /

I’m Erin Wong, an architectural designer from Hong Kong and a master’s graduate of Aalto University. My work is driven by a curiosity about how spaces are shaped through materials, making processes, and everyday use. I’m particularly interested in working with wood and natural materials, exploring how they interact with structure, technology, and human scale. Through both architectural and furniture projects, I approach design as a balance between conceptual thinking and hands-on experimentation. My approach values clarity, restraint, and careful attention to detail.
